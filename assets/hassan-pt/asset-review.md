## Grading Scale

- `A` Strong client-ready asset
- `B` Usable supporting asset
- `C` Archive/reference only, not preferred for client-facing use

## Notes

- Many female-patient treatment assets were graded down because the patient face is visible and earlier guidance was to avoid using that publicly.
- Male-patient cervical/shoulder assessment assets are the strongest client-facing treatment set.
- Prone upper-back treatment shots without visible face are generally usable.
- Workout demo videos are more appropriate for social/reference use than the core website.
